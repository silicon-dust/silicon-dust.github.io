    Ellipsis moves

    Design +Process Restricted

    When you want to build or modify something, the MC will tell you what you need to make it happen by picking as many options from the following list as makes sense :

    • It will take a 1, 2 or 3 actions to complete.
    • You will need a worskpace.
    • You will need to spend some cred to cover the basics.
    • You will need special materials, tools and/or data.
    • You will need extensive danger management measures.
    • You will need the help of ___.

    When you have everything you need, roll +Process.

    Success

    Just as planned, whatever you made, you made it right.

    Partial

    It's a beta, it works but pick 1 :

    • One of the features doesn't work properly. What is it?
    • It's more fragile than you wish. Add 2-use.
    • You need something more to make it work. The MC will tell you what. Add destroyed until you get it.
    • It works first try. Add dangerous.

    Miss

    You have a barely working prototype, it should do the job but it's unpredictable. When you use it, Overcome with +Process to see what happens.

    Gig +Rep

    When you're looking to make some money in your spare time, choose a faction with which you have a Rep of 0 or more and roll +Rep.

    Success

    They could really use someone with your talents right now. Tell us what they needed you for. You make 5-cred and +1 Rep.

    Partial

    You've got options, a well-paying job or something that will get you noticed. Choose if you make 5-cred or 3-cred and +1 Rep. Figure out the details of the gig you end up doing with the MC.

    Miss

    There's something they need done but it's problematic to say the least. The MC tells you what it is. If you do it, make 5-cred and lose -1 Rep with another faction. If you don't, you find a crappy gig elsewhere, make 3-cred and tell us why your character swore they're never doing that again.

    Investigate +Process

    When you investigate a person, group or location, roll +Process.

    Success

    Ask 3.

    Partial

    Ask 1.

    Miss

    Ask 1 anyway but the MC gives a complication.

    Questions :

    • Where is ___ ?
    • How is ___ secured ?
    • To whom ___ may be valuable ?
    • To whom or what is ___ related ?
    • Who owned or employed ___ ?
    • Are ___ and ___ related ? If yes, how ?
    • What doesn't fit about ___ ?

    Reshell +Trauma

    When you wake up in a new shell, roll +Trauma.

    Success

    It goes as well as it could, your Ellipsis action is refunded. What feels familiar about this shell?

    Partial

    It's the mother of all blackouts, something's off and you need to do go through the paces. What's the first thing you do to feel right in this shell?

    Miss

    This is not your body. Suffer 1-trauma. What's wrong and what do you have to do to yourself to fix it?

    Then, because it's an important event, take the spotlight for a bit to show the group what rebirth is like for you.

    If you're out of ideas, consider the following prompts:

    • "What's your first thought or impression when waking up ?"
    • "How does your character experience discontinuity ?"
    • "What about this shell feels different to you ?"
