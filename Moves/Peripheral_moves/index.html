    Peripheral moves

    Diverge +Forks

    At the beginning of the session, upon ellipsis or if the MC asks for it, roll the number of forks you currently have (your original self included).

    "Success"

    The writing is on the wall. Add +3 to your Divergence clock.

    "Partial"

    Small signs, unusual behaviors. Add +1 to the Divergence clock.

    "Miss"

    You're you and the other you's are you, too. keep narrative control. For now.

    Fork

    The first time you make a fork of yourself, you gain a Divergence clock with 4 segments unfilled. You can play that fork as if it's a full second character. Consider making a sheet for them if they start gaining XP or they reshell.

    Whenever that clock is full, for any reason, one of you has grown appart. Pick one of your forks, you lose narrative control over their actions and they become an NPC. How they act towards you and yours is up to the MC now. It's usually a mess, but it's not written in stone.

    When that happens, if you've still got forks left, reset the divergence clock to 2.

    Discord and Unity

    When you pay tribute or make yourself useful to a faction, gain +1 Rep with them.
    When what you did hurt a faction, remove -1 or -2 Rep with them depending on the damage done.
    When you publicly help a faction with enemies, lose -1 Rep with one of them.

    Favors +Rep

    When you approach a member of a faction looking for a favor, roll +Rep.

    Success

    They are feeling gracious, get 1 Favor no strings attached.

    Partial

    They'll help out, but you owe them. get 1 Favor right now but they'll ask something from you. If you don't do it, lose 1 Rep with them.

    Miss

    They get contractual. They ask something from you right now. If you do it, get 1 Favor. If you don't, lose -1 Rep with them.

    Favors :

    • Ask a question from the Investigate move.
    • Get a gear of value equal to their Tier or less.
    • Get a temporary squad or expert of their Tier.
    • Ask for a special device, service or vehicle.

    Resurrection insurance

    When your shell is destroyed and your resurrection insurance pays off...

    ... if you have a Lifestyle 0, get a basic infoshell.

    ... if you have a Lifestyle 1, 2 or 3, choose as many as your Lifestyle :

    • The new shell has the same augs (or of same value) as your previous one (else it has -tier-3 and the MC may choose to change some).
    • You choose if the shell has a downside: tracked, previous owner, unresponsive, spurious perception, addiction, wild controls, psycho-affective trouble, chronic pain, ...
    • You choose the shell's type (bioshell or synshell) and look.

    All unselected options are for the MC to detail. You may refuse the MC detailing an option by decreasing your Lifestyle by 1 for each option refused.

    Then you probably need to Reshell during the next ellipsis.

    Resist hacking +Process

    When you're getting hacked and warnings start popping up everywhere, roll +Process.

    Success

    You manage to contain the attack and you gather some useful data on the assailant.

    Partial

    Choose 1 :

    • You suffer 2-harm ap
    • One of your augs or connected items gets broken (MC's choice).
    • Your sensors saturates and you miss something important.
    • They get info on you and what you may know.
    • You're glitched : -1 ongoing until you can recalibrate.

    Miss

    MC chooses 1 :

    • You suffer 4-harm ap
    • You suffer 1-trauma
    • One of your augs gets destroyed
    • They forknap you.
    • Your shell is their puppet : Overcome with +Process to gain back control or not...
