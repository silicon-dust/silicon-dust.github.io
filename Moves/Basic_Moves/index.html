    Basic moves

    Endure/Overcome +Approach

    When you overcome an obstacle or push through danger, choose with the MC if you do it with :

    • Speed and power : roll +Kinesics.
    • Situational awareness and precision : roll +Sensors.
    • Quick predictions and brute force calculations : roll +Process.
    • Social graces and manipulation : roll +Memetics.

    Success

    You do it.

    Partial

    You do it but it's not pretty. You'll either get a mitigated result, a choice between two complications or an unforeseen consequence. Mc's choice.

    Boost/Disrupt

    When you help or impede another player and it's time to roll some dice, give them +-1d-forward, as appropriate. You are now entangled in the consequences of their move.

    Query

    When you query data, ask questions to the MC and they will answer you given the data contains this information. Querying the Grid is possible but will only yield public results.

    Analyze behavior +Memetics

    When you're analyzing someone's behavior throughout an interaction, roll +Memetics.

    Success

    Hold 3.

    Partial

    Hold 1.

    Miss

    Hold 1 but your target analyzed you as well (MC may ask questions).

    For the duration of the interaction you can spend your hold 1 for 1 to ask one of the following questions :
    - Are they honest ?
    - What do they want ?
    - What are they feeling right now ?
    - Which memes do they adhere to ?
    - How could I get them to ___ ?

    On a success or partial, you have +1d-forward if you act on the answers.

    Assess a situation +Sensors

    When you assess a situation, roll +Sensors.

    Success

    Ask 3.

    Partial

    Ask 1.

    Miss

    Ask 1 anyways but the MC will give you a soft complication.

    Questions :
    - What technology is at play here ?
    - What's a potential complication ?
    - What's hidden here ?
    - How ___ is vulnerable to me ?
    - What's the best way in / out / past / through ?
    - What's the biggest threat here ?

    On a success or partial, you have +1d-forward if you act on the answers.

    Convince +Memetics

    When you argue, fast talk or persuade someone to do something feasible or to believe something plausible, roll +Memetics.

    Success

    They do it or believe you until something tangible contradicts your reasoning.

    Partial

    They do it or believe you if you first promise to do something or give them a credible corroboration or warranty.

    Miss

    Deploy violence +Kinesics

    When you deploy violence, say what you want and roll +Kinesics.

    Success

    You inflict harm and choose 1 :
    - You suffer no harm.
    - You take definitive control of someone/something/some place but suffer harm.

    Partial

    You inflict harm, suffer harm and the MC gives a complication.

    Miss

    You suffer harm and the MC gives a complication.

    Get in touch +Memetics

    Once per session, you can declare a contact. Say who they are, what they do, where they usually do it and roll +Memetics.

    Success

    Anything for a friend. They owe you one, why?

    Partial

    Just business. They're around, but they don't work for free. Offer help or pay up.

    Infiltrate +Sensors

    When you're sneaking in places you don't belong, roll +Sensors.

    Success

    You go unnoticed and have +1d-forward.

    Partial

    You go unnoticed but the MC chooses 1 :

    • You attract minor attention.
    • You leave something behind.
    • You take something with you.
    • You have to overcome an obstacle.

    Miss

    You're detected but you have a small window to prevent the alert to be transmitted.

    Intimidate +Kinesics

    When you want someone to do something under threat of violence, tell them what you want them to do and what you'll do to them if they don't cooperate, then roll +Kinesics.

    Success

    They choose between :

    • Force your hand and suck it up.
    • Cave and do what you want.

    Partial

    They may also choose :

    • Run away.
    • Try to diffuse the situation.
    • Prepare for conflict.

    Suffer harm +Harm

    When you suffer harm, roll +Harm suffered.

    "Success"

    Choose 1 :

    • You're out of action : unconscious, trapped, lost, glitched or panicked.
    • One of your aug is destroyed, MC's choice.

    "Partial"

    Choose 1 :

    • You miss something.
    • You're put in a spot.
    • One of your aug or locomotion mode is broken (you say which).
    • Take -1d-ongoing until you can rest.

    "Miss"

    You endure the damage.

    If you have a total of 6 or more harm or if your shell dies, it's time to take a look at your Resurrection insurance.

    Suffer trauma +Trauma

    When you suffer trauma, add it to your total then roll +Trauma.

    "Success"

    You lose control of yourself (MC says what you do). Memories optional.

    "Partial"

    Choose 1 :

    • You have -1d-ongoing until you can chill out.
    • You miss something.

    "Miss"

    You internalize the trauma and push forward.

    In any case, the MC gives you a trauma complication.

    When you reach 6-trauma, you lose it. Your character may take a last stand but after that they become a NPC. They may still have an old backup of themselves with less trauma, who knows ?
