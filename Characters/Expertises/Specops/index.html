    Specops

    Specops is the art of infiltrating and exfiltrating secure locations and organizing deployment of violence.

    Specops moves

    Covering tracks +Process

    When you plant fake evidence to deflect investigation onto someone else, name who you're trying to frame, roll +Process.

    Success

    The investigation goes in the wrong direction buying you a lot of time. The framed party will suffer major consequences.

    Partial

    Same as a success but it won't last long and the framed party will suffer minor consequences.

    Miss

    It won't last long and you pissed off the framed party.

    Planning ahead

    You're always prepared. Start with 3 Preps. During ellipsis, replenish your Prep to 3.

    When you need something, you've already prepared for it, spend 1 Prep to :

    • Have a specific gear (2-cred max, you must pay the rest).
    • Have the services of a specific expert of tier-2.
    • Ask a question from the Investigate move.

    Psychological warfare +Kinesics

    When you attempt to influence the morale of your enemies by leaving evidence of violence while remaining undetected, roll +Kinesics.

    Success

    Choose if your enemies are impressed and overly cautious, scared and demoralised, or angry and careless.

    Partial

    Same as a Success but the MC chooses.

    Miss

    Prepare for the worst.

    Tactical leadership

    When you give orders during an operation, if they follow it, they have +1d-forward.

    Tactical retreat +Sensors

    When you need an escape route, roll +Sensors.

    Success

    You're out.

    Partial

    You're out but the MC chooses if you take something with you or leave something behind.

    Miss

    You get caught or trapped.

    Visions of life and death +Kinesics

    When you go into a battle, roll +Kinesics.

    Success

    Name someone who will die and someone who will live.

    Partial

    Name someone who will die or someone who will live.

    Miss

    You've got a bad feeling about it, you have -1d-ongoing during this battle.

    Character creation

    When you create your character at the first session, you get a starting move, a shell to customize and some gear and assets. Follow the guide below.

    When you acquire this expertise through an improvement, you only get a starting move. No shell, no gear.

    Starting moves

    You start with 1 move of your choice. Choose 1 at the front of this handout.

    Starting shell

    Your shell starts with +2 Kinesics (fill 2 boxes on your shell handout).

    Additionally choose 1:

    • +1 Process (max +2)
    • +1 Sensors (max +2)

    Additionally choose 2 augs below and copy them down in the Augs section of your Shell handout:

    • Ballistics softwares tier-1 : +1 to Deploy violence at range.
    • Close combat software tier-1 : +1 to Deploy violence in close combat.
    • Dead switch tier-1 : automatically deletes your mind at death.
    • EMP mantis vibroblades tier-1 : 6-harm emp hand
    • Integrated monofilament whip tier-1 : 4-harm ap mutilate dangerous hand/close

    Choose if your shell is a bioshell or a synthshell and fill in its look.

    Choose a Downside with the MC for this shell.

    Starting gear and assets

    Copy down your stuff in the Gear and Assets section of your Archetype handout.

    By default you get :

    • A daemon : falsifies most identification measures. Contrary to most people, what you do is not publicly available information. It may still be retrieved by motivated and competent individuals.
    • A weapon worth 4-cred. Choose 1 below or generate it:
      • Electrokatana : 5-harm emp hand
      • Stealthy machine pistol : 5-harm close/near automatic 2-ammo stealthy with an integrated grappling.
      • Ammofabber sniper : 5-harm near/far/away 2-ammo stealthy

    Additionally, choose 1 :

    • An armored wearable with thermo-optic cammo : 1-armor, +1 to Infiltrate.
    • Jammer : Can block all wireless communications in a close range. Everything there goes off-grid.
    • Trackers : 2-use, geolocates and monitors the surface it's been fixed on.

    Gigs

    As a special operative you have access to gigs such as physical infiltration in a secured location, bounty hunting, kidnapping, assassination, target extraction, ...
