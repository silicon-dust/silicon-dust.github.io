    Cybernetics

    Cybernetics is the art of designing, upgrading and integrating augmentations.

    Cybernetics moves

     Integrate

    When you have an aug, time, a place with the correct tools and a patient (cooperative or sedated), you integrate the augmentation to their shells. You can be your own patient.

    An eye for augs

    You can always ask "What augs do they have ?" without the need to roll for it.

    Augs design

    When you want to build or modify a specific aug, you may use the Design move to do it.

    Heal / Repair +Process

    When you have time and material to heal or repair a shell, roll +Process.

    Success

    You remove up to 3 tracks to their Harm Clock.

    Partial

    You remove up to 2 tracks to their Harm Clock.

    Miss

    You remove 1 track to their Harm Clock.

    Overclock +Process

    When you push the limits of what an aug can do, roll +Process.

    Success

    The augmentation overperforms up to a spectacular level for a short time.

    Partial

    Same as success but MC chooses 1:

    • The aug breaks just after.
    • You suffer 2-harm.
    • It goes even beyond, having unintended consequences.

    Salvage +Process

    When you have time, a shell with augs at your disposal and the correct tools, you may try to extract their augs, roll +Process.

    Success

    You extract augs with a good value (MC details).

    Partial

    You extract one aug (MC details).

    Miss

    You extract a basic aug (MC details) but you destroy the shell.

    Character creation

    When you create your character at the first session, you get a starting move, a shell to customize and some gear and assets. Follow the guide below.

    When you acquire this expertise through an improvement, you only get a starting move. No shell, no gear.

    Starting moves

    You start with 1 move of your choice. Choose 1 at the front of this handout. Other moves can be acquired as improvements.

    Starting shell

    Your shell starts with +2 Process (fill 2 boxes on your Shell handout).

    Additionally choose 1:

    • +1 Kinesics (max +2)
    • +1 Sensors (max +2)

    Additionally choose 2 augs below and copy them down in the Augs section of your Shell handout:

    • Additional motricity actuators tier-1 : Choose 1 : flight (wings, propeller, ...), swim (fins, propeller, ...), wall climbing (grappling, magnetic crampon, adhesive limbs, ...), ...
    • Fast healing / Autorepair tier-1 : Your body heals or repair itself fast. Whenever an hour or two passes, you erase all tracks on your Harm Clock.
    • Fractal digits tier-1 : A set of fractally dividing digits to manipulate matter up to the molecular level. Allows to use Nanotechnology provided you have relevant matter at hands.
    • Shapeshift mods tier-1 : You can change your "look" at will in the constraints of your general morphology. Synthshells can simulate bioshell looks this way too.
    • Terahertz optics tier-1 : You can see through walls.

    Choose if your shell is a bioshell, synthshell or infoshell and fill in its look. Note that an infoshell can't have +Kinesics and +Sensors augs.

    Starting gear and assets

    Copy down your stuff in the Gear and Assets section of your Archetype handout.

    You start with :

    • A lab with all the required tooling for cybernetics. Where is it ? Whose is it ?

    Additionally, choose 1 :

    • A custom weapon worth 3-cred.
    • A private backup infoshell with +2 Process.
    • A drone worth 1-cred.
