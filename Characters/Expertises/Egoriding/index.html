    Egoriding

    Egoriding is the art of interfacing with drones, vehicles and shells to control them in first person.

    Egoriding moves

    Drone egoriding

    When you egoride drones, use their +Kinesics and +Sensors instead of yours.

    Mechanics

    You may use the Design move to build or mod a drone or vehicle. You may repair drones and vehicles for free.

    Multiriding

    You may egoride 2 shells, drones or vehicles, without forking, at the same time. You may choose this move as an improvement again to control 3, 4, ... puppets at the same time.

    Shell egoriding +Process

    When you egoride a shell equipped with a puppeteer override, roll +Process.

    Success

    You take full control of their shell. You use their +Kinesics and +Sensors instead of yours. All below :

    • They will not remember what you did while egoriding them.
    • They will not try to take back control if you do something against their instinct and fundamental values. You need to Overcome with +Process to keep control when they do.

    Partial

    Same as success except that you choose only one.

    Vehicle egoriding +Process

    When you're egoriding a vehicle during a chase, roll your vehicle ... ?

    Success

    Hold 3 maneuvers while chasing.

    Partial

    Hold 1 maneuvers while chasing.

    Spend your maneuvers 1 for 1 :

    • You catch up with a prey.
    • You evade a predator.
    • You inflict harm to a target.
    • You avoid an obstacle.
    • You give +1d-forward or -1d-forward

    Character creation

    When you create your character at the first session, you get a starting move, a shell to customize and some gear and assets. Follow the guide below.

    When you acquire this expertise through an improvement, you only get a starting move. No shell, no gear.

    Starting moves

    You start with 1 move of your choice. Choose 1 at the front of this handout. Other moves can be acquired as improvements.

    Starting shell

    Your shell starts with +2 Process (fill 2 boxes on your Shell handout).

    Additionally choose 1:

    • +1 Memetics (max +2)
    • +1 Sensors (max +2)

    Additionally choose 1 aug below and copy them down in the Augs section of your Shell handout:

    • Additional motricity actuators tier-1 : Choose 1 : flight (wings, propeller, ...), swim (fins, propeller, ...), wall climbing (grappling, magnetic crampon, adhesive limbs, ...), ...
    • Integrated jammer tier-1 : Allows to block all wireless communications : everything in a close range becomes off-grid.
    • Stealth enhancements tier-1 : Sheets of epidermic metamaterials and silenced actuators giving a bonus of +1 to Infiltrate.

    Choose if your shell is a bioshell, synthshell or infoshell and fill in its look. Note that an infoshell can't have +Kinesics and +Sensors augs.

    Starting gear and assets

    Copy down your stuff in the Gear and Assets section of your Archetype handout.

    You start with :

    • Puppeteer overrides hand infinite 0-aug : Devices attaching to drones, vehicles and shells and providing an interface to egoride them.

    Additionally, choose 2 :

    • A drone worth 3-cred.
    • A vehicle worth 5-cred.
    • A launcher for your puppeteer overrides. Allows to implant them at a near range.
    • A +1-aug computation chip : +1 Process.
