    Services

    Reparation : 1-cred for each broken item.
    Healing : 1-cred for all tracks from 0 to 3. Past 4, its +1-cred for each additional tracks.

    Squads

    Squads are groups of people capable of rolling the basic moves with a specific stat. The stat they can roll depends on their type :

    • Security : +Kinesics (create it as a Gang).
    • Infiltrators : +Sensors.
    • Socialites : +Memetics.

    By default, they can roll all the basic moves using their stat type with a dice pool equal to their Tier. They can also roll other basic moves with a dice pool equal to 0. They also can Boost / Disrupt a move rolled with their type stat.

    It's possible to add an additional type to a squad by lowering their Tier by 1.

    Usually, squads have a Tier ranging from I to IV. A Tier V squad is possible but extremely rare.

    Experts

    Expert are individuals able to roll expertise moves from a single expertise. There type corresponds to their expertise : Expert Hacker, Expert Investigator, Expert Memegineer, ...

    By default, they may roll any of their expertise move with a dice pool equal to their Tier. They may also roll basic moves with a dice pool of 0. They may also Boost / Disrupt a move from their expertise.

    It's possible to add an additional expertise to an expert by lowering their Tier by 1.

    Usually, experts have a Tier ranging from I to IV. A Tier V squad is possible but extremely rare.

    Gangs

    A gang is an individual or group able to fight. This is a mechanic to judge the lethality of a violent encounter. It may be used by PCs if they have a squad of type gang and it may be used by the MC to generate an encounter.

    An encounter is an enemy or a squad that's going to fight.

    At Tier 0, an encounter has...
    - ... a 4 Harm clock
    - ... they inflict 2-harm hand close (near)

    For each tier :
    - Tough : +2 Harm clock
    - Brutal : +2-harm (can stack, max 6-harm)
    - Ranged : +far +away
    - Actions are On the Edge.
    - Armored : +1-armor (can stack, max 3-armor)

    If it comes to you in play that they may have other special tags, make a fortune roll with their Tier as dice pool.

    Praetorian Master (Tier V)
    Harm clock : 4
    Plasma rifle : 6-harm hand close near
    Armor : 3-armor

    Moiraï samuraï (Tier IV)
    Harm clock : 6
    Harm : 4-harm mutilate hand close near
    Armor : 1-armor

    Martian brawler (Tier II)
    Harm clock : 4
    Harm : 4-harm hand close
    Armor : 1-armor
