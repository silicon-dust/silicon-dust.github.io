    Influencer

    Moves

    Fame and Cancel

    The world is a stage and you're the main character. You're here for the fame. But exposing oneself to the crowd is also a risk of cancel. You need to navigate social cues and trends with caution or you will loose precious reputation.

    Fill 1 segment of your Fame clock when you shine on a publicized appearance, do something controversial, do a collaboration, or attack another influencer.

    Remove 1 segment of your Fame clock when you're publicly ridiculed, piss off sponsors or take to much time to make content.

    Fill 1 segment of your Cancel clock when you alienate your community, piss off sponsors, do something controversial or attack another influencer.

    Remove 1 segment of your Cancel clock when you do a apologetic content, please sponsors or deflect attacks to someone else.

    When the Fame change clock is full, your reach pierces the mainstream, there is a great party, you set a lasting trend, you're the main event for a time. Increase your faction's Tier by 1. In addition, if it makes sense, another faction may decrease by 1 Tier. Reset your Cancel clock to 0.

    When the Cancel clock is full, your community lashes back at you, your sponsors abandon you, everyone seems to turn its back on you, at least publicly and for a time. Decrease your faction's Tier by 1 and reset the Cancel clock.

    Favors

    You may not use the Favors move with your faction. Instead, each session, you may acquire as many free assets as your faction tier. You may still ask for Favors as usual with other factions.

    Publish content +Memetics

    When you want to create content, describe what it's about, which medium you use and how you're producing it. The MC will first tell you that you need:

    • Special authorization.
    • Special gear.
    • Transhuman resources: cast, designer, producer, ...

    When you release it or go live, roll +Memetics.

    Success

    Choose 3 :

    • It's a big success: increase your Fame by 1.
    • Attract sponsors and make 5-cred.
    • Initiate an aesthetic trend in this habitat.
    • Gain +1 Rep with a faction.
    • Provoke your followers to engage in an action : protest, party, investigate, ...

    Partial

    Choose 1 above and the MC chooses 1 below :

    • Loose -1 Rep with a faction (MC says which).
    • It backfires: +1 Cancel Clock.
    • Your followers do something reckless or toxic (MC says what).

    Benefits

    Leading a faction comes with benefits. Choose as many as your faction's Tier :

    • You have +1 when your Resurrection insurance activates.
    • You have +1 Lifestyle (without the stash to back it though).
    • You have a permanent squad of your Tier.
    • You have a permanent expert of your Tier.
    • You have access to +4-augs.

    Each time your faction increases or decreases in Tier, add or remove 1 more accordingly.

    Famous +Tier

    When you meet someone for the first time, roll +Tier of your faction.

    Success

    They're a fan. You've got +1d-ongoing with them.

    Partial

    They don't know who you are.

    Miss

    They hate your content.

    Life of the party

    You're always invited to private parties, conferences and the like. When you go, roll +Process.

    Success

    1 VIP of your choice is here too and eager to meet you.

    Partial

    1 VIP of your choice is here too.

    In any case, you always have a guestlist.

    My fans are everywhere

    Whenever you're in public, there's at least a fan that you can trivially convince to:

    • Create a distraction for you.
    • Introduce you to someone you're interested into.
    • Leak info to you.
    • Pass a message on your behalf.

    Parasocial exploitation

    When you use your most obsessive fans, ask them something they could do for you, they'll do it but in exchange, choose 1:

    • You must give them a personal and uncomfortable reward in exchange.
    • They will succeed, but get caught and suffer the consequences.
    • They bring you bad press: +1 on your Cancel clock.

    Sponsor

    Your faction has a higher Tier faction as its sponsor. It gives you a temporary +1 Tier as well as a temporary Lifestyle 3 (without the stash to back it though). You always need to further your sponsor's interests any time you publish content. In addition, your sponsor will ask things from you from time to time. If you don't give them what they want or come to have a negative Rep with them, you loose their sponsorship and all the associated bonuses. You may try to court another faction for sponsorship but they will ask something from you first. If your faction's Tier equals your sponsor's, you loose their sponsorship, you're too big of a fish now.

    XP and Improvements

    When you roll a highlighted stat, you gain 1 XP.

    When you spend 5 XP, you gain a new move from your archetype or your expertise(s). If you already had at least 5 improvements, you may also spend 5 XP to gain a new expertise. You only gain access to its moves this way, no starting gear.

    Lifestyle and Stash

    You start with a Lifestyle 1.

    • Lifestyle 0 : no food, no energy, no place.
    • Lifestyle 1 / Stash : 5-cred : enough resources to live and a basic place to stay.
    • Lifestyle 2 / Stash : 15-cred : luxe resources and a nice place.
    • Lifestyle 3 / Stash : 30-cred : the hyper-elite lifestyle.

    You may increase your lifestyle by storing cred in your stash.
