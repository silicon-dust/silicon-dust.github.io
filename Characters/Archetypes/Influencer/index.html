    The Influencer

    What is the Influencer about ?
    - Creating and diffusing content for its audience.
    - Art and entertainment in the future.
    - Position into and change the aesthetic of habitats and societies.
    - Getting famous and part of the social elite.

    Theme:
    - Elite life
    - "Illegal" activities
    - Socioculture
    - Exploration
    - Technology

    Medium:
    - Recorded or live XP.
    - Simulspace and AR narrative piece.
    - Simulspace and AR interactive piece.
    - Performance.

    Content

    What kind of content you're producing (choose 1 main but you're not restricted to it) :
    - XP of extreme sports, the elite life, "illegal" activities, exploration, ...
    - XP of creation performance of a media: music, 3D sculpture, ...
    - Simulspace and AR games and experiences.
    - Opinion pieces and sociopolitical analysis

    Fame and Cancel

    The world is a stage and you're the main character. You're here for the fame. But exposing oneself to the crowd is also a risk of cancel. You need to navigate social cues and trends with caution or you will loose precious reputation.

    Something is fundamentally wrong in this habitat and you're here to bring social change. At the first session, define what kind of social change you want to bring and ask the MC how this habitat is ripe for change: between 0 and 3 segments are prefilled on your Social change clock.
    Being a leader isn't easy, dissent grows in your ranks as you push your agenda and break your promises. Your Dissent clock represents the ramping divide in your faction and it begins empty.

    Fill 1 segment of your Social clock:

    • When you severely hurt your enemy's interests.
    • When you show the world the damage done by your enemy.
    • When a respected figure takes your side.

    Remove 1 segment of your Social clock:

    • When your faction's interests are severely hurt.
    • When damage done by yourself or your faction becomes public.
    • When a respected figure takes side against you.

    Fill 1 segment of your Dissent clock:

    • When your faction's interests are severely hurt.
    • When you break a pledge.
    • When you do something contrary to your faction's values.
    • When you associate with enemies.

    Remove 1 segment of your Dissent clock:

    • When you give your faction what they want.
    • When you remove internal dissension.

    When the Social change clock is full, you bring social change in this habitat : laws are passed, history is written, power is transferred, people riots, mentalities evolve, ... As a result, increase your faction's Tier by 1. In addition, if it makes sense, another faction may decrease by 1 Tier. Social change is not always a clean process, your Cause wins, but there may be costs to be paid or casualties: ask the MC. Finally, reset your Social change clock and define what's your next step.

    When the Dissent clock is full, your faction breaks up or is brutally attacked, your authority crumbles or you're betrayed. Decrease your faction's Tier by 1 and reset the Dissent clock.

    Favors

    You may not use the Favors move with your faction. Instead, each session, you may acquire as many free assets as your faction tier. You may still ask for Favors as usual with other factions.

    Benefits

    Leading a faction comes with benefits. Choose as many as your faction's Tier :

    • You have +1 when your Resurrection insurance activates.
    • You have a permanent squad of your Tier.
    • You have a permanent expert of your Tier.
    • You have access to +4-augs.

    Each time your faction increases or decreases in Tier, add or remove 1 more accordingly.

    Famous +Memetics

    When someone already knows you (you say or the MC says), roll +Memetics.

    Success

    You say what they've heard of you.

    Partial

    You say something they've heard of you and the MC adds something.

    Miss

    The MC says what they've heard of you.

    Sponsor

    Your faction has a higher Tier faction as its sponsor. It gives you a temporary +1 Tier. Your sponsor will ask things from you from time to time. If you don't give them, you loose their sponsorship and all the associated bonuses. You may try to court another faction for sponsorship but they will ask something from you first. If your faction's Tier equals your sponsor's, you loose their sponsorship, you're a bigger fish now.

    Publish content

    When you want to create content, describe what it will look like. The MC will first tell you that you need :

    • Special authorization.
    • Special gear.
    • Transhuman resources: cast, designer, producer, ...

    Success

    Choose 3 :

    • Attract sponsors and make cred (how much ?).
    • Change the aesthetic in the habitat.
    • Gain new followers : +1 Following Clock.
    • Gain +1 Rep with a faction.
    • Provoke your followers to engage in an action : protest, party, compile information, ...

    Partial

    Choose 1 above and the MC chooses 1 below :

    • Loose -1 Rep with a faction (MC says which).
    • Your followers do something reckless or toxic (MC says what): +1 Cancel Clock.

    Sponsor

    You have a rich sponsor. They give you a +1 Lifestyle while you do what they want. Create them as an faction but you can't use the Faction move with them.
