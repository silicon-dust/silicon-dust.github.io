    Async

    Archetype introduction... Focus on the following: What are you playing? Why is it interesting?

    Async moves

    Async +Async

    You awakened to be an async. Time flows chaotically for you, letting you unsure of its true ordering but capable of understanding the faintest threads of causality.

    As an async, you have a unique stat, +Async, starting from +0. You can spend an improvement to get +1 Async (up to a maximum of +4).

    Once each session or when you increase Async, roll +Async:

    Success

    The MC chooses an occurence and you answer the questions.

    Partial

    Choose an occurence and the MC answers the questions.

    Miss

    The MC chooses an occurence and answers the questions.

    Occurences (cross out up to four if you don't want them to happen at all):

    • You awaken as an async. What happens ?
    • Someone else learns you're an async ? Who ?
    • Your async capacities are affected. By what/who ? How ?
    • You change in a radical way. How so ? Why ?
    • You lose something or someone important. What ? Why ?
    • Someone who has been looking for you finds you. Who ?
    • You meet another async. Who ? What happens ?
    • You are experimented upon ? By whom ? How ? Take 1 trauma.
    • Someone understood the truth of your existence ? Who ? How do they react ?
    • You understand the nature of the async field. How ?
    • You understand the origin of your capabilities. How ?
    • ________________________________________________
    • ________________________________________________

    Initially, an occurence isn't placed in your timeline, you're unsure of it's in the past, the present or the future. At any moment, you can place an occurence on your timeline by stating when it happened relative to now and other already placed occurences. When you do so, the Mandela effect and Time compression moves can be triggered if you have them.

    When at least 10 occurences have been placed in your timeline, whenever you want, choose 1:

    • You find a way to propagate in the async field, leaving your shell, locality and present altogether. Make a new character.
    • You always knew that at some point your asyc abilities would leave you and you would be left here and now. You loose the Async archetype and all its moves. Make a new character or choose a new archetype for this one.
    • You realize that your async abilities were given to you to achieve a grand purpose. When you achieve it, you loose the Async archetype and all its moves. Make a new character or choose a new archetype for this one.
    • One of your vision turns out false, creating a paradox that threatens reality itself. The MC creates a Paradox threat. If it fills out completely, the universe will just stop existing. The clock is ticking...
    • Upon your sacrifice, you can rewrite an important part of history or force something important to happen in the future. What will your sacrifice be for ?

    Specimen

    Asyncs are rare and their powers misunderstood. Various interests would capture and study you if they knew about your capacities.

    Asynchronous vision +Async

    When you desynchronise yourself from here and now, ask something and roll +Async.

    Success

    MC gives you a clear perception and you know if it's in the past, the present or the future.

    Partial

    MC gives you impressions and you don't know when it's happening.

    Miss

    MC gives you a horrific vision, prepare for the worst.

    The vision necessarily has happened, is happening or will happen.

    Butterfly effect +Async

    When you feel the threads of causality and want something to happen, ask the MC what should you do to make it happen and roll +Async.

    Success

    The MC tells you of something relatively simple to do to make it happen.

    Partial

    The MC tells you of something complex and 2 unintended side-effects. If it happens, throw a coin to see which happens.

    Miss

    Something you do will trigger the chain of events that will cause the inverse to happen or terrible unintended consequences.

    Everything is connected +Async

    When you ponder the causal links tying things together, roll +Async.

    Success

    Say two things are connected. MC says how they are connected.

    Partial

    Choose something. The MC chooses something else and say how they are connected.

    Miss

    The MC chooses two things and say how they are connected. Prepare for the worst.

    The MC describes how you perceive the connection.

    I am a leaf in the stream of creation

    When you Deploy violence, use +Async instead of +Kinesics. In addition you seem to miraculously avoid harm. When you wear no armor, you have 2-armor.

    Just a feeling

    When you Analyze behavior or Assess a situation, use +Async instead of +Memetics.

    Mandela effect

    When you place an occurence on your timeline, you can change something that happened during this occurence. Your entire timeline is rewritten such that this always has been, is or will be.

    Time compression

    When you place an occurence on your timeline, you can make an ellipisis move without requiring to shift to ellipsis mode.

    Character creation

    Starting moves

    You start with the Async and Specimen moves as well as a move of your choice.

    Expertise(s)

    Choose 1 expertise and follow it's creation rules to the end, this will give you your expertise moves, your shell, your starting credits and your gear.

    Background

    Your current predicament makes knowing your background difficult at the present (???) time.

    Links

    Ask 2 questions to other players and let them answer if they want to:

    • Who did I wrong ? You have no recollection of it.
    • Who did I wrong ? They have no recollection of it.
    • Who is my good friend ? They have no recollection of it.
    • Who's secret I know ? They don't know how you know.

    Factions

    Choose a faction with which you start with +1 and another with which you start with -1. Explain why.

    Name

    Finally, choose a name: ...

    XP and Improvements

    When you roll a highlighted stat, you gain 1 XP.

    When you spend 5 XP, you gain a new move from your archetype or your expertise(s). If you already had at least 5 improvements, you may also spend 5 XP to gain a new expertise. You only gain access to its moves this way, no starting gear.

    Lifestyle and Stash

    You start with a Lifestyle 1.

    • Lifestyle 0 : no food, no energy, no place.
    • Lifestyle 1 / Stash : 5-cred : enough resources to live and a basic place to stay.
    • Lifestyle 2 / Stash : 15-cred : luxe resources and a nice place.
    • Lifestyle 3 / Stash : 30-cred : the hyper-elite lifestyle.

    You may increase your lifestyle by storing cred in your stash.
