    Exolog

    The Exolog is all about understanding aliens, their societies and their technology. When you play an exolog, it means aliens will play an important part in your story.

    Moves

    Exolinguistic

    Each time you spend time studying records of alien language or have time to interact with a cooperative live alien, fill 1 from first to last. You can now understand these aspects from records or live communication. Start again if you're studying another alien language.

    • Maths and logic.
    • Physics.
    • Technology.
    • Society and politics.
    • Culture and arts.
    • Philosophy.

    Exoengineering

    Each time you spend time studying alien technology, fill 1 from first to last. Start again if you're studying another alien technology.

    • How to crudely enable it (danger).
    • What is its purpose.
    • How to finely control it.
    • What is its cultural significance.
    • How to you exploit it.

    Exoarcheology

    Each time you spend time studying alien structures and constructions, fill 1 from first to last. Start again if you're studying another alien structure.

    • How is it laid out and what it is made of.
    • What was its primary purpose.
    • What is its historical and cultural context.
    • What are its secrets.

    Exobiology

    Each time you spend time studying a live alien specimen, fill 1 from first to last. Fill 2 if you dissect the specimen. Start again if you're studying another alien specimen.

    • Metabolism and environmental needs.
    • Sensory capacities.
    • Special capacities.
    • Reproductive cycle and life stages.
    • Evolutionary context.

    Exopsychology

    You can use the Analyze behavior and Convince moves on an alien. It requires that you fully understand its language with Exolinguistic.

    Exopolitics

    You can use the Favors move with alien factions. It requires that you fully understand its language with Exolinguistic.

    Reverse engineering +Process Ellipsis

    When you have fully understood a piece of alien technology (using Exoengineering) and you have the resources to reverse engineer it, roll +Process.

    Success

    You find a safe, economic and reliable production process. How will it impact transhumanity ? Do you release it or withold knowledge ?

    Partial

    You find a costly and dangerous production process. It is not ready for propagation yet.

    Miss

    Something goes terribly wrong during the research.

    Integrate +Process Ellipsis

    When you have fully understood a piece of alien technology (using Exoengineering) and you have the resources to integrate it in your shell, roll +Process.

    Success

    You integrate it succesfully. Work out with the MC what it looks like and does. Add it to your augs and tell us how you feel about integrating alien technology ?

    Partial

    Same as success but there is a side effect to it or you loose something important in the process.

    Miss

    Something goes terribly wrong during the research, your shell is destroyed, ...

    Exoconditionning +Process Ellipsis

    When you have fully understood a non-intelligent alien lifeform (using Exobiology) and you have the resources to train it, roll +Process.

    Success

    You pet the lifeform. The bond is strong but they still have instincts.

    Partial

    You create a contact with the lifeform but it has its own needs and preferences.

    Miss

    The lifeform cannot be domesticated and you learn it the hard way.

    Character creation

    Starting moves

    Choose 2 starting move.

    Expertise(s)

    Choose 1 expertise and follow it's creation rules to the end, this will give you your expertise moves, your shell, your starting credits and your gear.

    Background

    Choose 1 background from the list, answer its questions.

    In addition, answer these questions too:

    • Nobody starts by studying alien lifeforms. You are a world-class expert in your field. What was your original specialty ?
    • What exology breakthrough are you known for ?
    • Who was instrumental in this discovery ?
    • What about the alien species you studied was particularly weird ?

    Links

    Ask 2 questions to other players and let them answer if they want to:

    • Who was in the team that helped with my breakthrough ?
    • Who here is a fan of my work ?
    • Who here thinks aliens aren't real ?
    • Who here dreams of meeting an alien ?

    Choose a faction with which you start with +1 and another with which you start with -1. Explain why.

    XP and Improvements

    When you roll a highlighted stat, you gain 1 XP.

    When you spend 5 XP, you gain a new move from your archetype or your expertise(s). If you already had at least 5 improvements, you may also spend 5 XP to gain a new expertise. You only gain access to its moves this way, no starting gear.

    Lifestyle and Stash

    You start with a Lifestyle 1.

    • Lifestyle 0 : no food, no energy, no place.
    • Lifestyle 1 / Stash : 5-cred : enough resources to live and a basic place to stay.
    • Lifestyle 2 / Stash : 15-cred : luxe resources and a nice place.
    • Lifestyle 3 / Stash : 30-cred : the hyper-elite lifestyle.

    You may increase your lifestyle by storing cred in your stash.
