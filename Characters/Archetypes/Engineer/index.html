    Engineer

    Dev notes

    The engineer has an Omnitool and a special relationship with it. The Omnitool may be upgraded with advancements.

    What kinds of things the Omnitool allows to do ?

    • Generate stuff on the fly.
    • Repair stuff.
    • Modify and control stuff.
    • Make expertise move you don't have.
    • As a weapon.
    • As a drone.
    • As a vehicle.
    • As a psychosurgery tool.
    • As sensors.

    Base range (the default range for its functionalities) : close/near

    Upgrades :
    - Electrokinetic manipulator : Can manipulate things at range as if you were there.
    - Plasma cutter : +breach
    - Range upgrade : +far then +awayFoam gun : allows to create rough structures and block passageways.
    - Repair : Repairing gear costs nothing. You can also repair complex devices and machinery.
    - Sensors : +1-Sensors (choose 2 or 3: chemical composites sorter, THz feed, EM scanner, ...)
    - Trackers : Can generate and project trackers and bugs.

    Actions:
    - Build
    - Repair
    - Modify
    - Destroy

    Domains :
    - Electronic
    - Flesh
    - Nanotech
    - Exotic tech

    Advancements :
    - Fabber : Allows you to absorb matter and spurt gear on the fly.
    - Integration : Integrated as an aug.
    - Autonomous Omnitool : Can be a drone.
    - ??? : Can be a vehicle.
    - Omniweapon : reconfigures as a weapon on the fly. There are base tags and you can choose additional tags on the fly.
    - Mass destruction : You can setup your Omnitool as a mass destruction weapon. It will destroy it but you can rebuild it as usual.

    Base moves : Upgrade your omnitool.

    XP andÂ Improvements

    When you roll a highlighted stat, you gain 1 XP.

    When you spend 5 XP, you gain a new move from your archetype or your expertise(s). If you already had at least 5 improvements, you may also spend 5 XP to gain a new expertise. You only gain access to its moves this way, no starting gear.

    Lifestyle and Stash

    You start with a Lifestyle ....

    • Lifestyle 0 : no food, no energy, no place.
    • Lifestyle 1 / Stash : 5-cred : enough resources to live and a basic place to stay.
    • Lifestyle 2 / Stash : 15-cred : luxe resources and a nice place.
    • Lifestyle 3 / Stash : 30-cred : the hyper-elite lifestyle.

    You may increase your lifestyle by storing cred in your stash.
