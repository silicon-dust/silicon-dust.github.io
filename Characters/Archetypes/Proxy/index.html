    The Proxy

    The Proxy is a specialised freelance operative hopping from habitat to habitat, either fleeing or pursuing their past. Pick the Proxy if you always want to be the most highly qualified person in the room and don't want to be tied too deeply to whatever mess might be happening here.

    Character creation

    _When you create your Proxy during the first session, choose TWO Expertises, answer your Background questions, ask your Link questions and pick a Name.

    Expertises

    Choose 2 expertises and follow their creation guide with one exception: Instead of getting your gear, cred and shell twice, pick the one you prefer from both Expertises. You can mix and match: pick the shell from your first expertise, the gear from the second and the creds from the first if you wish.

    Background

    Answer yourself (Pick 1 and answer the last):

    • You were part of a criminal organization, who were they and what did they do ?
    • Which corporation or authority did you use to serve ? How did you come to be in their service and what made you leave ?
    • You grew up in a fringe society outside of the Consortium. What made them fringe, and how did you lose contact with them ?
    • You were a mindless animal, forcefully uplifted to transhuman sapience. Which group was behind the operation? Did you know their purpose ?
    • You might have been someone, but your broken ego hold barely any memory of your past. But there is that one dream... What is it ?
    • Whatever you were, are you looking for your past, or is your past hunting you?

    Links

    Ask the table (Pick 2):

    • Who here employed or employs me ?
    • Who here is my contact ?
    • Who here might know of my past ?
    • Who here is the most likely to betray me ?

    Name

    Did you keep your old name or did you have to change it to something new?

    XP and Improvements

    When you roll a highlighted stat, you gain 1 XP.

    When you spend 5 XP, you gain a new move from your archetype or your expertise(s). If you already had at least 5 improvements, you may also spend 5 XP to gain a new expertise. You only gain access to its moves this way, no starting gear.

    Lifestyle and Stash

    You start with a personal Lifestyle 0 and an empty Stash. But your Privileges gives you a Lifestyle 3 while you're an Exec.

    • Lifestyle 0 : no food, no energy, no place.
    • Lifestyle 1 / Stash : 5-cred : enough resources to live and a basic place to stay.
    • Lifestyle 2 / Stash : 15-cred : luxe resources and a nice place.
    • Lifestyle 3 / Stash : 30-cred : the hyper-elite lifestyle.

    You may increase your lifestyle by storing cred in your stash.
