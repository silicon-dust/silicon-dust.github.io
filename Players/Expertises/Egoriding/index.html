    Egoriding

    What droning is about :
    - Egoride a shell.
    - Projecting you ego in the drone.
    - Building/Modifying vehicles.
    - Building/Modifying robots.
    - Piloting vehicles.
    - Modifying something to make it a drone.

    Gear

    • A workshop with tools, materials and the necessary infrastructure to build, mod and repair drones and vehicles. You may use Drone builder and Drone modder in your workshop.
    • A drone.

    Additionally choose 2 :
    - A +1-aug computation chip : +1 Process.
    -

    Egoriding moves

    When you get this expertise choose 1 move between Combat egoriding or Stealth egoriding.

    Shell egoriding

    You may egoride shells by implanting a puppeteer override into a shell.

    Combat egoriding

    When you egoride a drone, a vehicle or a shell equipped with a puppeteer override implant, you may use +Kinesics moves with your ride's +Kinesics bonus.

    Stealth egoriding

    When you egoride a drone, a vehicle or a shell equipped with a puppeteer override implant, you may use +Sensors moves with your Drone +Sensors bonus.

    Chaser [+Kinesics]

    When you're in a chase while egoriding, roll your ride's +Kinesics.

    Success : Hold 3 while chasing :

    • You catch up with a prey.
    • You evade a predator.
    • You inflict harm to a target.
    • You avoid an obstacle.
    • You give +1 or -1 to a roll.

    Partial : Hold 1 while chasing.

    Miss : Prepare for the worst.

    Drone builder

    You may use the Design move to build, repair or mod a drone. Generate it using the Drone generation rules or add mods appropriately.

    Mechanics

    You may use the Design move to build, repair or mod a vehicle. Generate it using the Vehicle generation rules or add mods appropriately.

    Epic Egoriding move

    Whenever you try to simultaneously egoride an additional ride, roll +Process.

    Success : You do it.

    Partial : You do it but have -1d-ongoing.

    Miss : Prepare for the worst.
